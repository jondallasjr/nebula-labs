Frequently Asked Questions

Can I change plans anytime?

Yes. Upgrade or downgrade your plan at any time. Changes take effect at your next billing cycle.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major credit cards, ACH transfers, and wire transfers for annual plans. Enterprise customers can discuss custom payment terms with our sales team.

Is there a free tier?

We offer a 14-day free trial of the Professional plan for all new users. No credit card required to start.

What happens to my data if I cancel?

Your data remains accessible for 30 days after cancellation. You can export it anytime. We also provide a data deletion option upon request.

Do you offer volume discounts?

Yes. Enterprise customers and high-volume users can negotiate custom pricing. Contact our sales team to discuss your specific needs.

What's included in support?

Starter includes email support. Professional includes 24/7 priority support with < 1 hour response time. Enterprise includes a dedicated account manager.
