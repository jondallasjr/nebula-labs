Security Architecture

Nebula Labs employs defense-in-depth security practices across our entire platform and infrastructure.

Encryption & Data Protection

  • In Transit: TLS 1.3 for all data in motion, with certificate pinning for critical endpoints
  • At Rest: AES-256 encryption for all stored data, with HSM-managed key storage
  • Key Management: Automated key rotation, audit logging, and strict access controls
  • Backup Security: Encrypted backups with geographic redundancy and regular restoration testing

Infrastructure Security

  • Cloud Infrastructure: Deployed on AWS with VPC isolation and security group controls
  • Network Security: WAF protection, DDoS mitigation, and continuous monitoring
  • Access Controls: Multi-factor authentication, role-based access control (RBAC), and principle of least privilege
  • Vulnerability Management: Regular scanning, automated patching, and bug bounty program

Compliance Standards

  • SOC 2 Type II: Annual audits confirming security, availability, and integrity controls
  • ISO 27001: Information security management system certification
  • GDPR: Full compliance with data protection regulations and user rights
  • HIPAA: Available for healthcare organizations with BAA agreements
  • Industry Standards: Compliance with relevant regulations for financial and regulated sectors

Access & Authentication

  • Employee Access: Background checks, NDA agreements, and mandatory security training
  • Customer Authentication: Single Sign-On (SSO), API key management, and OAuth 2.0 support
  • Audit Logging: Complete audit trails for all access and changes
  • Session Management: Automatic timeout, secure token handling, and CSRF protection

Data Privacy

  • No Model Training: Customer data is never used to train our models or improve our service without explicit consent
  • Data Deletion: Secure deletion on request with cryptographic verification
  • Data Portability: Export your data in standard formats at any time
  • Privacy Controls: Granular controls over data sharing and retention policies

Incident Response

  • 24/7 Monitoring: Continuous monitoring and alerting for security events
  • Incident Team: Dedicated security team on-call for rapid response
  • Communication: Transparent communication with affected customers within 24 hours
  • Post-Incident: Root cause analysis and preventive measures implementation

Frequently Asked Questions

Where is my data stored?

Your data is stored in AWS data centers within your selected region. We support US, EU, and APAC regions. Data never leaves your chosen region without explicit permission.

Can you use my data to train models?

No. Nebula Labs never uses customer data for model training, service improvement, or any other purpose without explicit written consent. Your data remains completely confidential.

What if there's a security incident?

We monitor for incidents 24/7. In the unlikely event of a breach, we notify all affected customers within 24 hours and provide full details and remediation steps.

Do you offer HIPAA compliance?

Yes. HIPAA-compliant deployments are available for healthcare organizations. Contact our team to discuss Business Associate Agreement (BAA) requirements.

Can I audit your security?

Yes. Enterprise customers can request SOC 2 Type II reports, penetration test summaries, and conduct their own security assessments with our cooperation.

How do you handle data deletion?

Upon request, we securely delete all your data within 30 days. Deletion is cryptographically verified and logged in our audit trail.
