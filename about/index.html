Our Story

Nebula Labs was founded by a team of ML engineers and product designers who spent years building AI systems at tech companies. We watched brilliant mid-market companies struggle with the same ML challenges we'd solved, but they lacked the resources and expertise to build solutions themselves.

We realized the problem wasn't their ambition or intelligence‚Äîit was access. They needed ML infrastructure designed for their scale, not stripped-down versions of enterprise tools built for 10,000-person engineering teams.

That's why we built Nebula Labs. We combined our deep technical expertise with real empathy for the constraints of growing companies. The result is a platform that's powerful enough for complex use cases, simple enough for small teams to implement quickly, and priced fairly for mid-market budgets.

Today, hundreds of companies use Nebula Labs to unlock ML capabilities that were previously out of reach. We're proud to be part of their growth stories.
